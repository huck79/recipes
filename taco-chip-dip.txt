Ingredients:
 - 8 oz cream cheese
 - 8 oz sour cream
 - 1 packet taco seasoning
 - 1 lb lean ground turkey
 - 2 cups shredded taco cheese blend

 Directions:
  1. Brown ground turkey in large frying pan.

  2. Combine cream cheese, sour cream, in a large mixing bowl. Mix
     until smooth.

  3. Spread mixture into large cake pan or deep cookie sheet.

  4. Chop browned turkey until fine (Taco Bell style meat) and spread
     over cream mixture.

  5. Top with shredded cheese.

  6. Serve cold with totrilla chips. Enjoy!!!
